Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a korean martial art which you can learn about by watching youtube videos or reading wikipedia! I'm just writing this entry to tell you why I like it! To start off, nothing brings clarity of mind like getting punched lovingly in the face by a good friend. But on a more serious note, martial arts train the body as well as the mind. It teaches humility and respect. Taekwondo has 4 disciplines:

  • Tul: a style practice in which practicioners run a certain pattern.
  • Sparring: it's exactly what it sounds like. We wear protection on hands and feet and then have free sparring (semi-contact).
  • Power breaking: Using different techniques to break wooden or specially designed pvc planks.
  • Hosinsul: self-defence. Going from freeing oneself 'gently' to incapacitating or restraining opponents.
